# 👋 Welcome to SIM IT Club

We’re the SIM Information Technology Club (ITC) — a student-run community where techies, curious minds, and “I’ll-learn-as-I-go” folks gather to build cool stuff, learn useful things, and just vibe together. ✨  

Our mission? **Make tech fun, relevant, and accessible for everyone.**  
No gatekeeping. No stress. Just growth and community. 🚀  

---

## 💡 Who We Are

We’re simply a group of ambitious students who wanted to create a platform for SIM techies to **learn, grow, and make an impact**.  

Our community is all about curiosity, collaboration, and building cool things together — no matter your background or starting point. 🚀

## 📚 Programs & Initiatives

### ITCamp: Frontendamentals
A **3-month interactive workshop** for subcommittee members, where you’ll learn to build sleek, interactive websites from scratch. 🌟

**What you’ll get:**
- ✅ Hands-on skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Tailwind, and Vercel
- ✅ Guided sessions with experienced mentors
- ✅ A portfolio-ready mini project
- ✅ A supportive community of tech enthusiasts

No coding background? No problem — we’ll guide you step by step! 💡

## 🔓 Roles Available

### 💡 President
- Establish the club’s vision and steer the club’s overall initiatives and priorities
- Leads the EXCO team, facilitate meetings and ensures effective team collaboration
- Works closely with SIM Student Development Advisor and external stakeholders for formal affairs and collaborations
- Drives long-term planning and strategy for ITC’s future developments

### 💡 Vice President
- Assists the President in managing internal operations,
- Bridges cross-functional collaboration between different departments
- Ensures strong team cohesion for the club and maintains welfare alongside the President

### 💡 General Secretary
- Manage club documentation, internal communications, and monitor the club's email regularly to ensure timely correspondence
- Coordinate meeting scheduling, prepare meeting minutes, and track deadlines to ensure tasks are completed on time
- Assist in administrative tasks, including drafting proposals, post-event reports, and building forms for registration and other club needs
- Handle HR-related matters, including managing and coordinating the general subcommittee

### 💡 Finance & Logistics Secretary
- Manage the club locker and maintain an inventory of supplies to avoid unnecessary purchases and ensure resources are used efficiently
- Maintaining accurate financial records, invoices, receipts, and expense reports.
- Monitoring budgets, identifying discrepancies, and preparing financial summaries for management.
- Handling accounts payable/receivable, billing, and basic bookkeeping tasks.
- Coordinating purchase orders, tracking shipments, and managing documentation between suppliers and internal departments.

### 💡 Technical Director (Internal)
- Develop and improve ITCamp Curriculum
- Develop and Quality check for lessons content and its exercises with exco team
- Coordinate internal project development
- Manage internal project showcase

### 💡 Technical Director (External)
- Maintain ITClub Website by updating blogs, announcements, etc.
- Supervise and manage external workshops (e.g. GitHub workshop, React workshops, etc.)
- Manage technical subcommittees
- Manage tech-related projects for events (e.g. Hackahton landing pages, voting website, games, etc.)

### 💡 Marketing Director
- Managing the organization's social media presence, poster design, and outreach campaigns.
- Formulating and implementing strategies to expand public awareness and community engagement.
- Directing and overseeing the marketing subcommittee to ensure cohesive and effective promotional efforts.
- Monitoring campaign performance, identifying target audiences, and refining messaging to maximize the organization's outreach impact.

### 💡 Partnerships & Innovation Director
- Managing and cultivating external partnerships to ensure alignment with the club's goals and values.
- Sourcing and securing sponsorships that provide meaningful support and resources for the club.
- Building and maintaining long-term relationships with partners that bring mutual benefit to all parties involved.
- Identifying new collaboration opportunities and innovative approaches to expand the club's network and partnership portfolio.
